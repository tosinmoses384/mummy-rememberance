# Deploy to Netlify (so you can share the link with family)

You said you're already logged into Netlify. Do this:

1. **Open:** https://app.netlify.com/drop

2. On your Mac, open Finder and go to this folder:
   `DOCUMENTATION_VARIETIES/0_MY_MUMMY_1_YEAR_REMEMBRANCE/netlify-deploy`

3. Drag the **entire** `netlify-deploy` **folder** onto the Netlify drop zone (the big "Drag and drop your site output folder here" area).

4. Wait a few seconds. Netlify will give you a link like:
   `https://something-random-123.netlify.app`

5. Copy that link and share it with your brothers and sisters. When they open it on their phone, they'll see the plan and can tap to tick.

**Optional:** In your Netlify dashboard you can change the site name to something like `mummy-remembrance` so the link is easier to remember or share.
